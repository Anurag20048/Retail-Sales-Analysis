# 📊 Retail Sales Data Analysis

## 🔹 Project Overview

This project performs end-to-end data analysis on a retail sales dataset to uncover key business insights related to sales trends, product performance, customer behavior, and profitability.

The goal is to help businesses make data-driven decisions by identifying profit drivers, loss-making areas, and customer segments.

---

## 🔹 Objectives

* Analyze sales trends over time
* Identify high-performing categories and products
* Detect loss-making sub-categories
* Understand the impact of discounts on profit
* Segment customers based on purchasing behavior

---

## 🔹 Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn

---

## 🔹 Dataset

Superstore Sales Dataset

---

## 🔹 Key Analysis Performed

### 📈 Sales Trend Analysis

* Analyzed monthly sales trends to identify growth patterns and seasonality
* Observed fluctuations in sales across different time periods

---

### 📊 Category & Sub-Category Analysis

* Technology category generates the highest profit
* Furniture category shows lower profitability despite high sales
* Tables and Bookcases identified as loss-making products

---

### 💸 Discount vs Profit Analysis

* Strong negative relationship between discount and profit
* High discounts lead to reduced profitability and losses

---

### 👥 Customer Analysis

* Identified top customers contributing the highest revenue
* Observed that a small group of customers drives most of the sales

---

### 🧠 Customer Segmentation (RFM)

Customers were segmented into:

* High Value
* Medium Value
* Low Value

Based on:

* Recency (last purchase)
* Frequency (number of orders)
* Monetary (total spending)

---

## 🔹 Key Insights

* High sales do not always result in high profit
* Certain products consistently generate losses
* Discount strategy significantly impacts profitability
* A small percentage of customers contribute a large share of revenue

---

## 🔹 Business Recommendations

* Reduce discounts on loss-making products
* Focus on high-value customers for retention
* Promote high-profit products
* Optimize pricing strategies across categories
